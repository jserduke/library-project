Inventory toString() appends:
Item #int:
General Format of toString Media Types:
Always has "Media Info{ID:...;Title:...;Publisher:...;Genre:...;Total Quantity:...;QuantityAvailable:...;}"
in front, followed by Media Type Line:
"Book:[Author:...;DDNumber:...;ISBN:...]\n"
"DVD:[AgeRating:...;RunTime:...]\n"
"BOARD_GAME:[AgeRating:...;Min Players:...;Max Players:...;Game Length:...]\n"

e.g.) Item #1:Media Info{ID:1;Title:The Lord of the Rings;Publisher:Allen & Unwin;Genre:Fantasy;Total Quantity:5;Quantity Available:3}BOOK:[Author:J.R.R. Tolkien;DDNumber:123.456;ISBN:987654]
